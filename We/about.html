The Green Bean was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate green bean enthusiasts who wanted to create a community to share their love of this incredible legume.

Our mission is to:

  • Educate people about the nutritional benefits and culinary versatility of green beans
  • Showcase exceptional green bean dishes and the people who create them
  • Advocate for greater appreciation and consumption of green beans

We believe that green beans are an underappreciated wonder of the vegetable world, and we're on a mission to change that. Whether you're a longtime green bean devotee or just discovering their charms, we welcome you to join our community.
